Passion From Jony Ive

2023-06-20 - Written by: Filippo

I am fascinated by this man.

He is a gifted designer who gave us fabulous products as Apple’s chief design officer. Some of his most outstanding works:

  • Apple park
  • Apple stores
  • iPhones
  • MacBook-Air

These images represent him perfectly. The products and designs above are the outcomes of his and his team’s effort. I believe the images communicate well his remarkable talent. His genius might be the result of Jonathan Ive’s passion for design and details but also his charming speaking ability. I find his ability to deliver concepts effortlessly to his audience astonishing. Everyone is capable of enjoying his creations and his philosophy of design. It is a pleasure listening to him; I always feel inspired and inventive after hearing his talks.

Jony Ive’s talks I found inspiring:

  • Tribute to Steve Jobs
  • AirPods 1
  • 2021 CCA Commencement Honorary Doctorate Address
  • Conversation with Graydon Carter
  • 2018 Hawking Fellow

I explore the most interesting “tools” that Jonathan Ive uses to express his passion.

  • His voice

    It is the tool that conveys the message. He speaks with a deep voice. It almost seems the passion is gently flowing out from deep within. The pauses emphasize crucial words. These words echo with the audience. This way, the ideas and the concepts are: heard, understood, felt and internalized by the audience. The cadence of his talks is consistent and calm. Ive is in no hurry: the timing of each speech is perfect. He has a rich vocabulary and uses each word accurately to express ideas precisely. The words chosen are simple yet powerful. He also has superb diction that ensures he is never unheard or mistaken.

  • His presence

    is composed, calm and collected. He delivers the concepts with confidence and enthusiasm. His clothing style defines a unique personality. It is simple but with tremendous attention to detail. His presence exactly matches his voice.

  • His aura

    Ive is a master of his field. He has won countless awards and titles. He is over-qualified, so he can concentrate on rehearsing the speech. He practices to a level of absurd perfection while maintaining a genuine tone.

Great creators should communicate to spread ideas worth spreading. I’m writing this post to provide a starting point for readers that want to improve their communication.
