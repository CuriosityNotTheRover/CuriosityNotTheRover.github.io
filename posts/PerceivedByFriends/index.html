How do my Friends Perceive Me?

2023-01-20 - Written by: Filippo

I ask my close friends questions about me, and they answer. Nothing unexpected until here. The beauty of this simple exchange resides in the fact that they are honest because they are real friends. They are different from me; they have their ideas and view the world differently. They can see me from the outside. They probably have a perspective on who I am and how I behave differently from mine.

The kind of questions I asked my friends are: Have I behaved this way recently? Is this behavior showing frequently? Have I improved on this thing recently? What things trigger some of my behavior? Their answers often surprise me. Knowing them, I expect some answers but receive completely different answers. These questions always lead to a frank conversation if I listen to their sincere responses without showing attitude. Listening to my friends gave me an idea of what I could do better.

Unfortunately, I cannot perceive myself from the exterior or find a person who is my replica. These two things create this unique situation. I can’t experience myself from the outside, and I can’t even search for someone like me to tell me how I am. I have accepted this fact even though the desire to see me from the outside remains incredible.

Sometimes, I feel that the questions I ask leave them perplexed. It may seem that I obsess too much about how I am perceived. I am genuinely interested to hear things that I cannot experience directly, and how my behavior is perceived is one of them. I want to stress the importance of having sincere friends and asking them for feedback and advice. You can discover your blind spots.
