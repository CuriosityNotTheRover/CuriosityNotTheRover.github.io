Passion From Jony Ive

2023-06-20 - Written by: Filippo

This man is fascinating. He is a gifted designer, delighting us with the design of fabulous products as Apple’s chief design officer.

Examples of his most outstanding contributions:

  • Apple park
  • Apple stores
  • iPhones
  • MacBook-Air

These images perfectly represent him. His design philosophy and his remarkable talent fill these pictures.

His genius may bloom from his passion and the cure of details. But what amazes me is his ability to deliver articulate concepts effortlessly to the audience. His charming and subdued speech delivers his message perfectly. Each word has a clear and precise purpose. Words fit perfectly in the phrases. Each phrase is at the exact point in the discourse. It seems that no words can improve his speech.

Everyone can enjoy hearing his ideas as much as seeing and touching his creations. It is a pleasure listening to him. He always manages to inspire me. I feel inventive after hearing his talks. His passion is pervasive.

Inspiring Jony Ive’s talks I enjoy listening to:

  • Tribute to Steve Jobs
  • AirPods 1
  • 2021 CCA Commencement Honorary Doctorate Address
  • Conversation with Graydon Carter
  • 2018 Hawking Fellow

Intrigued by how Jony Ive expresses his passion, I analyzed some recurring elements.

  • His voice

    It’s the medium carrying the message. It’s the most relevant element. Jony speaks with a deep voice. I always get the impression that his passion gently flows from deep within. The pauses emphasize crucial observations. Words consistently echo with the audience. This way, the ideas are heard, understood, felt, and internalized by the audience. His thoughts spread like a virus. The cadence of his talks is consistent and calm. Ive is in no hurry: the timing of each speech is perfect. He has a rich vocabulary and uses each word accurately to express ideas precisely. The words chosen are simple yet powerful. He also has superb diction that ensures he is never unheard or mistaken.

  • His presence

    It is composed, calm, and collected. Jony delivers the concepts with confidence and enthusiasm. His clothing style portrays a unique personality. It is simple, like the words, but with tremendous attention to detail, like his work. His presence exactly matches his voice and his persona.

  • His aura

    Ive is a master of his field. He has won countless awards and titles. If an over-qualified person exists for this job, it’s him. His status precedes him everywhere.

Great creators communicate to spread ideas worth spreading. I’m writing this post to provide a starting point for readers who want to improve their communication.
